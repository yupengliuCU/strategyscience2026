The Program

Five sessions, four rooms.
Twenty themes, ninety papers.

The program runs across two days in Boulder, with five 90-minute parallel sessions distributed across four rooms in the Koelbel Building and the Engineering Center.

Presentation format Each parallel session runs 90 minutes. Presentations are 15 minutes in 4-paper sessions and 12 minutes in 5-paper sessions, with the remaining time for Q&A.
