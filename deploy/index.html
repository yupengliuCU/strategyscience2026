Welcome

From the
local committee.

Strategy Science has, in just under a decade, established itself as an essential voice in our field – an outlet for innovative work that takes seriously the central questions of strategy.

This year's program reflects that ambition. Across two days you will see ninety papers organized around twenty themes that span what strategy scholars are working on right now: how AI is reshaping foresight and evaluation, what platforms do to competition, why human capital and place still matter, and where the boundary between the firm and the public sits today.

We hope you feel inspired and have a wonderful time in Boulder.

Janet Bercovitz · Tony Tong · Jeff York
